This project analyzes SmartMart Retail Company’s sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional trends, and discount impact to support strategic decision-making. As a team of TechCrush Data Analysts, we provided insights to optimize sales performance and improve customer strategy.
The analysis answered 15 key business questions including:
Total Sales, Profit & Quantity Sold
Sales & Profit Trends (Monthly & Yearly)
Top Products by Season
Customer Segment Performance
Discount Impact on Profitability
Regional Sales & Profit Analysis
Top Customers by Order Frequency
Shipping Mode Efficiency
